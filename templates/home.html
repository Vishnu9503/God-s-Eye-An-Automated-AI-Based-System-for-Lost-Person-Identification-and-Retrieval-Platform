About Us

Utilizing advanced facial recognition and GPS integration to facilitate missing person identification and location.

AI for Reunification

Welcome to our platform dedicated to aiding in the search for missing persons. We are committed to leveraging technology to assist in reuniting families and communities during times of distress.

Our team comprises passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, including technology, humanitarian aid, and community service.

Through the integration of facial recognition technology and GPS tracking, we strive to streamline the process of identifying and locating missing individuals.

Our goal is to empower users with the tools and resources needed to facilitate swift and effective search efforts.

Why Choose Us

Cutting-edge Technology

Our platform utilizes state-of-the-art facial recognition algorithms and GPS integration to streamline the identification process of missing persons, ensuring accuracy and efficiency in search operations.

Expert Team

Our team comprises skilled professionals with backgrounds in AI, data science, and software engineering, ensuring the development and implementation of robust technical solutions to address the complex challenges of missing person identification.

Data-driven Approach
